Matches Table/Collection:

Primary Key: match_id (Auto-incrementing unique identifier)
Columns:
date (Date)
home_team_id (Foreign Key referencing the Teams table)
away_team_id (Foreign Key referencing the Teams table)
home_score (Integer)
away_score (Integer)
tournament_id (Foreign Key referencing the Tournaments table)
city (String)
country_id (Foreign Key referencing the Countries table)
neutral (Boolean)
Shootouts Table/Collection:

Primary Key: shootout_id (Auto-incrementing unique identifier)
Columns:
match_id (Foreign Key referencing the Matches table)
winner_id (Foreign Key referencing the Teams table)
first_shooter_id (Foreign Key referencing the Teams table)
Goalscorers Table/Collection:

Primary Key: goal_id (Auto-incrementing unique identifier)
Columns:
match_id (Foreign Key referencing the Matches table)
team_id (Foreign Key referencing the Teams table)
scorer (String)
own_goal (Boolean)
penalty (Boolean)
Teams Table/Collection:

Primary Key: team_id (Auto-incrementing unique identifier)
Columns:
team_name (String)
Tournaments Table/Collection:

Primary Key: tournament_id (Auto-incrementing unique identifier)
Columns:
tournament_name (String)
Countries Table/Collection:

Primary Key: country_id (Auto-incrementing unique identifier)
Columns:
country_name (String)
